Common Questions

Can Viltreon delete my emails?

No. We only request permissions to modify labels. We cannot and will not permanently delete your messages.

How fast is the sorting?

Near-instantaneous. We use live push notifications from Gmail, so an email is categorized milliseconds after it arrives.

Do I need to leave a tab open?

Not at all. Viltreon runs entirely in the background on our servers.
